# Creator Performance Attribution Service

## Purpose
The Creator Performance Attribution Service provides an interface for tracking and analyzing revenue streams, channel performance, and product performance metrics related to content creators. This service aids in understanding revenue attribution across various channels and products.

## Usage
The service is designed to facilitate the retrieval and analysis of performance metrics for creators, track revenue across different streams, and assess the effectiveness of various channels and products. This can be used in scenarios such as marketing analysis, revenue reporting, and platform performance optimization.

## Parameters/Props

### RevenueStream
- `id` (string): Unique identifier for the revenue stream.
- `creatorId` (string): Identifier for the creator associated with the revenue.
- `channelType` (string): Type of revenue channel (e.g., subscription, one-time, commission, royalty, sponsorship).
- `productId` (string, optional): Identifier for the product generating revenue.
- `amount` (number): Amount of revenue generated.
- `currency` (string): Currency of the revenue.
- `timestamp` (Date): Date and time when the revenue was generated.
- `metadata` (Record<string, any>): Additional data related to the revenue stream.

### ChannelPerformance
- `channelType` (string): Type of the channel.
- `totalRevenue` (number): Total revenue generated through the channel.
- `transactionCount` (number): Number of transactions processed.
- `averageOrderValue` (number): Average value of orders.
- `conversionRate` (number): Conversion rate for the channel.
- `growthRate` (number): Growth rate of the channel’s revenue.
- `topProducts` (ProductPerformance[]): List of top performing products in this channel.

### ProductPerformance
- `productId` (string): Unique identifier for the product.
- `productName` (string): Name of the product.
- `revenue` (number): Revenue generated by the product.
- `units` (number): Number of units sold.
- `averagePrice` (number): Average selling price of the product.
- `conversionRate` (number): Conversion rate for this product.
- `channelBreakdown` (Record<string, number>): Revenue breakdown by channel.

### TimePeriod
- `start` (Date): Start date of the analysis period.
- `end` (Date): End date of the analysis period.
- `granularity` (string): Time granularity (e.g., hour, day, week, month, quarter, year).

### AttributionModel
- `type` (string): Attribution model type (e.g., first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay, position-based).
- `window` (number): Attribution window in days.
- `weights` (Record<string, number>, optional): Custom weights for channels in the attribution model.

### GrowthTrend
- `metric` (string): The metric being analyzed.
- `period` (TimePeriod): The time period for analysis.
- `currentValue` (number): Current value of the metric.
- `previousValue` (number): Previous value of the metric.
- `growthRate` (number): Computed growth rate.
- `trend` (string): Describes the trend (increasing, decreasing, stable).
- `forecast` (number[], optional): Forecasted values for future periods.
- `confidence` (number): Confidence level in the forecast.

## Return Values
The service returns aggregated performance metrics on revenue streams, channels, and products, including insights into revenue growth, conversion rates, and product performance. The output is structured to support data-driven decisions.

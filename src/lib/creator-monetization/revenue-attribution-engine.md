# Creator Revenue Attribution Engine

## Purpose
The Creator Revenue Attribution Engine is designed to analyze and determine the revenue contributions of various user interactions (touchpoints) across different channels for content creators. This engine applies attribution models to assign credit for conversions based on user engagement, thereby providing insights into which touchpoints are most effective in driving revenue.

## Usage
To use the Revenue Attribution Engine, integrate it within your analytics setup. It will track user interactions, process revenue conversions, and produce attribution results based on customizable models.

## Parameters / Props

### Interfaces

- **Touchpoint**
  - `id` (string): Unique identifier for the touchpoint.
  - `creatorId` (string): The ID of the content creator.
  - `userId` (string): The ID of the user who interacted.
  - `contentId` (string): The ID of the content interacted with.
  - `channel` (string): The platform/channel of interaction.
  - `type` ('view' | 'click' | 'share' | 'like' | 'comment' | 'subscribe' | 'purchase'): The type of user interaction.
  - `timestamp` (Date): When the interaction occurred.
  - `value` (number): The value representation of the interaction.
  - `metadata` (Record<string, any>): Additional data related to the interaction.
  - `sessionId` (string): ID for the interaction session.
  - `referrer?` (string): Optional referrer information.

- **RevenueConversion**
  - `id` (string): Unique identifier for the revenue event.
  - `creatorId` (string): The ID of the creator.
  - `userId` (string): The ID of the user making the revenue-generating interaction.
  - `amount` (number): Amount of revenue generated.
  - `currency` (string): Currency type of the revenue.
  - `source` ('subscription' | 'tips' | 'merchandise' | 'sponsorship' | 'affiliate' | 'ad_revenue'): Source of the revenue.
  - `timestamp` (Date): When the revenue was generated.
  - `metadata` (Record<string, any>): Additional information regarding the revenue event.
  - `transactionId` (string): Unique identifier for the transaction.

- **AttributionModel**
  - `type` ('first_touch' | 'last_touch' | 'linear' | 'time_decay' | 'position_based' | 'data_driven'): Type of attribution model.
  - `parameters` (Object): Custom specifications for the model, depending on the `type`.

- **AttributionResult**
  - `conversionId` (string): Identifier for the conversion.
  - `touchpoints` (Array<Object>): List of touchpoints contributing to the conversion, each with:
    - `touchpointId` (string): ID of the touchpoint.
    - `attributionWeight` (number): Weight of the touchpoint in the attribution.
    - `attributedRevenue` (number): Revenue attributed to the touchpoint.
  - `totalRevenue` (number): Total revenue generated by the conversion.
  - `conversionPath` (Array<string>): Path of touchpoints leading to conversion.

### RevenueAttributionAnalytics
- `totalRevenue` (number): Total revenue tracked.
- `attributedRevenue` (number): Revenue assigned to identified touchpoints.
- `topPerformingContent` (Array<Object>): List of top content items, each with:
  - `contentId` (string): ID of the content.
  - `attributedRevenue` (number): Revenue attributed to the content.
  - `touchpointCount` (number): Number of touchpoints for the content.
  - `conversionRate` (number): Rate of conversions per interaction.
- `channelPerformance` (Array<Object>): Performance metrics by channel, including attributes like `channel`, `attributedRevenue`, `touchpointCount`, and `averageConversionTime`.
